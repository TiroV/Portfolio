EP Penitence

Eras of the Dragon's Heart

Return To Home

My EP I built as part of a contest in which I entered a Music Jam to create as many pieces as possible relating to a certain theme. The theme of the contest anything that pertains to a Dragon -- My direction with my chosen sound was the differing eras and emotions that go into being a creature that lives for possibly forever. Each song in the EP are based upon a single work, with variations and differences in progression and percussion to suit each different mood and passion. I hope you enjoy it, and that it gives you a lense into the kind of atmospere I enjoy creating.
